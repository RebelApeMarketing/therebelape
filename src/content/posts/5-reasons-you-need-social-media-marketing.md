Social media has completely changed the game for advertising.

In fact, **97% of marketers say social media is now critical for all businesses**. By spending as little as six hours per week on social platforms, you can increase brand recognition — and even boost sales.

Some people still ask: do you really need [social media marketing](https://therebelape.com/blog/create-social-media-marketing-campaign/)?

Yes. You absolutely do. Here’s why.

---

## Social Media Marketing Strategies

---

## Increase Brand Awareness

A recent [Pew Research Center study](https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/2018/03/01/social-media-use-in-2018/) found that:

- **68% of American adults use Facebook**
- **78% of people aged 18–24 use Instagram**
- **45% are on Twitter**
- Even **37% of adults 65+** use social media

If this many people are spending time on social platforms, your business should be there too.

Implementing a strong [social media strategy](https://therebelape.com/blog/branding-strategies-in-marketing/) increases brand recognition by placing your business in front of a broad audience. Creating profiles, sharing content, and encouraging engagement introduces your brand to new networks of potential customers.

The more people who know about your business, the better.

---

## Engage With Your Customers

Social media is one of the best tools for **direct customer engagement**.

When you interact with customers on social platforms, you create two-way communication. This helps you understand their needs, answer questions, and build trust.

The more you engage with your audience, the higher your chances of conversion. Social media allows you to build real relationships and organic connections — not just impressions.

---

## Boosting Your Website SEO

[Search engine optimization](https://therebelape.com/seo/) (SEO) is an inbound marketing strategy that helps your website rank higher in search results.

SEO allows you to reach people *while they’re actively searching* for your products or services. It also improves website usability, speed, and navigation.

While social media itself isn’t a direct ranking factor, it supports SEO by:

- Driving traffic to your site
- Increasing brand searches
- Expanding content reach
- Supporting link-building opportunities

---

## Enhance Brand Loyalty

Many business owners focus too much on follower count.

In reality, **a smaller group of loyal, engaged followers is far more valuable** than thousands of random ones.

Brand loyalty leads to better engagement, stronger trust, and higher conversion rates. When people feel connected to your brand, they’re more likely to choose you — and recommend you to others.

Social media is one of the best tools for nurturing that loyalty over time.

---

## Cost Effective

Social media marketing is one of the most cost-effective forms of advertising available.

Creating profiles on most platforms is free, and **84% of marketers say they spend as little as six hours per week on social media** while still seeing measurable results.

Even paid social advertising on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter is relatively affordable compared to traditional advertising channels.

With as little as one hour per day spent creating and sharing content, businesses can begin seeing real traction.

---

Social media marketing is here to stay.

The longer you wait to take advantage of it, the more visibility, traffic, and conversions you leave on the table. When done right, social media marketing can lead to more customers, more engagement, and long-term business growth.

The sooner you start, the sooner you’ll see results.
