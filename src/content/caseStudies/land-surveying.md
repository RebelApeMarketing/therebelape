## The Problem

This land surveying company was technically excellent but had zero online presence. They faced:
- Complete dependence on referrals and repeat clients
- No way to reach new homeowners or developers
- Missed opportunities from people searching online
- No tracking of where leads came from
- Inconsistent workload and revenue

As a specialized B2C service, they needed to be visible when homeowners and property owners were actively searching for surveying services.

## The Solution

We focused on the fastest path to qualified leads: Google Local Service Ads.

### Google Local Service Ads Setup
- Completed Google Screened verification process
- Set up service area targeting
- Created compelling business profile
- Optimized for high-intent service categories
- Implemented dispute-free review strategy

### Call Tracking Implementation
- Set up dedicated tracking numbers
- Implemented call recording for quality assurance
- Created lead qualification criteria
- Built dashboard for real-time reporting
- Tracked calls through to closed revenue

### Profile Optimization
- Gathered and showcased customer reviews
- Added license and insurance documentation
- Created professional photos of team and work
- Highlighted response time and service areas
- Continuously updated availability

### Lead Qualification Process
- Trained team on handling LSA calls
- Created scripts for common questions
- Implemented follow-up system
- Tracked conversion rates
- Refined targeting based on call quality

## The Results

In just 4 months:

- **$60,200 in tracked revenue** from LSA leads
- **177 qualified calls** received
- **62 projects booked** (35% close rate)
- **$340 average cost per booked project**
- **4.2x return** on ad spend
- **4.8-star rating** maintained

## The Impact

The consistent lead flow allowed them to:
- Maintain full crew schedules year-round
- Expand service area with confidence
- Reduce dependence on referrals
- Plan equipment purchases and hiring
- Build emergency fund from predictable revenue

## Key Takeaways

1. **Local Service Ads Work for Service Businesses**: For licensed service providers, LSAs provide immediate visibility
2. **Reviews Drive Results**: Maintaining high ratings increased call volume by 40%
3. **Call Tracking Is Essential**: Knowing exact ROI allowed confident budget increases
4. **Speed Matters**: Responding to LSA calls within 5 minutes doubled close rates
5. **Quality Over Quantity**: 177 qualified calls beat 1,000 unqualified website visits
6. **Trust Signals Convert**: Google Screened badge and license verification increased booking rates by 28%
